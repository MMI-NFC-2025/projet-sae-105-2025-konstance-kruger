ABOUT

My name is Konstance KRUGER, I am 19 years old and I am studying in my first year of the BUT MMI program in Montbéliard.

You could say I am creative; I like exploring everything related to visual creation and originality.

Since I was a child, dance has been part of my life, whether it is modern jazz, contemporary, break, classical and more. I have always loved combining it with my creativity, especially when I reached high school. During those years I found a new hobby: freely creating scenarios, setting up projects about topics or feelings that inspire me, and editing videos in a very free way, sometimes to tell myself a story.

After spending years watching YouTube videos for hours, I decided to create my own because I increasingly felt it was something meant for me. It became a way to do what I love through video editing. I try to keep that positive energy that editing gives me in everything I do.

Last year, I had the chance to travel for three weeks to the United States. This experience opened my mind and made me want to discover even more of the world and new possibilities. For now, I do not know exactly what I want to do later, but I know I want to create, express myself and inspire others.

I created this website as part of a school project. But I also want to raise awareness about the struggles faced by women in fields that were originally considered to be “reserved” for men, especially in science. Many women have been discriminated against in the scientific world, which is not normal. That is why, through this site, I want to both inform and entertain, speaking about this topic in a fluid and engaging way, with a design that can encourage young people to read and learn more about it.

Konstance Kruger
